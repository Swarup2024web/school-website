Our Programs

Comprehensive educational offerings for all levels

Grade Level Programs

Early Childhood Program

Grades: Pre-K to K

Our early childhood program focuses on play-based learning, social development, and foundational academic skills. We create a nurturing environment where young learners explore, discover, and develop confidence.

  • Play-based curriculum
  • Social-emotional learning
  • Language and literacy
  • Early math concepts

Elementary School

Grades: 1 to 5

Our elementary program builds strong foundations in core subjects while fostering creativity and critical thinking. We emphasize both academic excellence and character development.

  • Integrated curriculum
  • Science and mathematics
  • Language arts and literature
  • Arts, music, and PE

Middle School

Grades: 6 to 8

The middle school years are transformative. Our program provides advanced academics, leadership opportunities, and guidance to help students navigate this important transition.

  • Subject specialization
  • STEM programs
  • Leadership development
  • Career exploration

High School

Grades: 9 to 12

Our high school program prepares students for college and beyond through rigorous academics, AP courses, internships, and mentorship. We focus on developing well-rounded global citizens.

  • AP and honors courses
  • College preparation
  • Internship programs
  • University counseling
